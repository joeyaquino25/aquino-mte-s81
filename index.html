BUSBAR TRUNKING SYSTEMS

Extraordinary quality and superior performance

A new creation at work for smart infrastructures and green energy distribution to power the world. Our busbar trunking systems support low-voltage electrical power distribution with aluminium and copper conductor options, serving applications from 800A to 6300A with compact sandwich design and reduced voltage drop.

Features

All metal parts of the joint block are anti-corrosion treated (By GEOMET 321 which is applied to protect fasteners and many other metallic parts from corrosion) to prevent any unforeseen chances of increasing operating temperature cause by corrosion.

Performance assurance

Engineered for dependable operation with quality-focused standards and support.

Certified compliance

Aligned with IEC certifications and vibration test requirements for demanding environments.

Lightweight aluminium casing

Physically strong profile extrusion with enhanced heat dissipation and corrosion protection.

Machine insulation process

Automated insulation of conductors for consistency, sealing integrity, and product quality.

Fire-resistant design

Self-extinguishing insulation materials and robust phase separation design for safety.

Quality-controlled manufacturing

Automated production and digital quality control for consistent finished products.

Why choose this system

Our busduct system delivers safe, efficient, and reliable power distribution for commercial and industrial applications. It is designed for easy installation, long-term durability, and consistent electrical performance. Built to meet modern infrastructure demands, it provides a practical solution for organized and space-saving power management.

Key Advantages

  • Reduced voltage drop during full-load transmission
  • Anti-corrosion treatment on key metal parts
  • Reduced installation and investment cost
  • High mechanical resistance and environmental durability
  • After-sales engineering support and performance assurance
